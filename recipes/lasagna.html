Black Bean Lasagna II

Lasagna with a Southwest twist. This can be frozen unbaked and kept for up to a month. Simply thaw in refrigerator overnight and bake as directed. Tastes even better reheated the second day!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped red bell pepper
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese (freshly grated)½ cup frozen corn kernels, thawed
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 (16 ounce) can refried black beans
  • 2 ¾ cups canned tomato sauce
  • ½ cup salsa
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro,divided
  • 1 ½ cups cottage cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • 8 ounces Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  • ¼ cup sliced ripe olives
  • 8 sprigs fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Coat a large skillet with non-stick cooking spray, and place over medium heat.
  3. In a large bowl, combine cottage cheese, ricotta, sour cream, shredded Monterey Jack cheese and remaining 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  4. Coat a 9x13 inch casserole dish with non-stick cooking spray. Arrange 3 of the cooked lasagna noodles in the bottom of the dish
  5. Cover, and bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes. Garnish with sliced black olives and sprigs of cilantro.
